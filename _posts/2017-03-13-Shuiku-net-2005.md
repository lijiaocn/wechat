         </span>
</p>
<p style="line-height:150%">
<span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:楷体">

<span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:楷体">

<p style="line-height:150%">

         </span>
</p>
<p style="line-height:150%">
<span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:楷体">
          指点你几招，让你到红尘中一谱传奇。
         </span>
</p>
<p style="line-height:150%">
<span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:楷体">
          列子御风而行。“隐士”过着神仙般的生活，出世孤立，却依然对红尘保持巨大的财富和影响力。

</p>
<p style="line-height:150%">
<span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:楷体">
</span>

<p style="line-height:150%">
<span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:楷体">
</span>

<span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:楷体">

</p>

<span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:楷体">

</span>

<p style="line-height:150%">

         </span>
</p>
<p style="line-height:150%">
<span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:楷体">
          你必须和红尘保持非常紧密的联络，必要时出手一二次。给自己的财富加个零。这样你才不会被时代淘汰。

<span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:楷体">

<span style="font-size:15px;line-height:150%;font-family:仿宋;color:green">

<span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:楷体">
</span>

<span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:楷体">
</span>
</p>

</span>

<p style="line-height:150%">
<span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:楷体">

<p style="line-height:150%">
<span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:楷体">

<span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:楷体">

</p>
<p style="line-height:150%">
<span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:楷体">

</p>
<p style="line-height:150%">
<span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:楷体">

<p style="line-height:150%">
<span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:楷体">

<p style="line-height:150%">
<span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:楷体">

</p>
<p style="line-height:150%">
<span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:楷体">
</span>
</p>
<p style="line-height:150%">
<span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:仿宋;color:#7030A0">
          你需要做什么么？你什么也不需要奋斗。
         </span>
</p>
<p style="line-height:150%">
<span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:仿宋;color:red">
          时代在推动着你。
         </span>
<span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:仿宋;color:#7030A0">

         </span>
</p>
<p style="line-height:150%">
<span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:楷体">
</span>
